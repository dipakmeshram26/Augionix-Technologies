Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

At Augionix Technologies, safeguarding the privacy of our visitors and clients is of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the rigorous standards we maintain to ensure your data remains secure.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our clients and site users. The information collected may include:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and company name when provided voluntarily via our contact form.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and anonymous usage stats collected via cookies and log files.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data based on legitimate business interests, including:

  • Responding to inquiries submitted through our contact and demo requests.
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage behavior to optimize user experience.
  • Securing our systems against unauthorized access and malicious activities.

3. Data Protection and Cybersecurity

As a cybersecurity firm, we implement military-grade protection mechanisms. We utilize end-to-end encryption, network firewalls, and rigorous access controls to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use standard cookies to improve navigation and analyze traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some interactive features of our site may not function fully as a result.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to external parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under regulations like GDPR or CCPA to access, modify, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact our Security Officer at secure@augionix.com.
